Jorge Drexler

Jorge Drexler is a Uruguayan singer-songwriter, musician, and composer known for his poetic lyrics and fusion of Latin American folk music, pop, and electronic elements. Born in 1964 in Montevideo, he initially trained as a doctor before fully dedicating himself to music. He gained international recognition in 2005 when he won an Academy Award for "Al otro lado del río", the theme song from "The Motorcycle Diaries." His music explores themes of love, identity, and science, combining melodic sophistication with profound lyricism.

Diamante Electrico

Diamante Eléctrico is a Colombian band formed in 2012, known for blending rock, funk, and electronic elements with bold lyrics and infectious rhythms. Over the years, they have evolved from raw garage rock to a more experimental and groovy sound. The band has won multiple Latin Grammy Awards and collaborated with artists like Adrián Quesada (Black Pumas) and Leonel García. Their music is dynamic, energetic, and constantly evolving, making them one of the most innovative bands in Latin America.

Sleep Token

Sleep Token is a British band that blends alternative metal, ambient, R&B, and progressive rock, creating a unique and atmospheric sound. The band is led by the mysterious figure Vessel, with all members remaining anonymous. Their music features haunting melodies, heavy riffs, and emotional lyrics, exploring themes of love, devotion, and spirituality. Since their debut, Sleep Token has gained a dedicated following, praised for their cinematic compositions and captivating live performances. Their albums, including Sundowning (2019) and Take Me Back to Eden (2023), showcase their genre-defying approach and evolving artistry.

The Home Team

The Home Team is an American pop-punk and alternative rock band known for their energetic sound, catchy melodies, and fusion of rock with funk and R&B influences. Formed in Seattle, Washington, they gained recognition with their 2018 album Better Off, but their 2021 release, Slow Bloom, truly defined their unique style. Their music blends upbeat instrumentals, soaring vocals, and groovy rhythms, setting them apart in the modern rock scene. With infectious hooks and dynamic performances, The Home Team continues to grow a passionate fanbase.
